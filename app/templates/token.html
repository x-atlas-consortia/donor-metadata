Application tokens



To obtain a new authorization token,

  1. Log in to the appropriate consortium application:
    • HuBMAP: the Ingest UI
    • SenNet: the SenNet Portal
  2. Open the developer tools for your browser--e.g., in Chrome, View-> Developer-> Developer Tools
  3. In the Application menu of Developer Tools, look in the Storage section.
  4. Obtain the value of the globus_groups key in the info cookie. The location of the cookie depends on the application:
    Application Location Example
    HuBMAP local storage
    SenNet cookies

    The actual location of the cookie information may be different in your brower's tool.

The info cookie in SenNet is base64-encoded. Decode the cookie to view its contents.
